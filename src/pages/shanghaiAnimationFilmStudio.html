Shanghai Animation Film Studio

History

Shanghai Animation Film Studio (abbreviated as Shanghai Animation) is one of the oldest animation studios in China, founded in April 1957 and located at 618 Wanhong Road, Changning District, Shanghai.

Shanghai Animation has created numerous classic animated works with its unique artistic style and superb production techniques, such as "Havoc in Heaven", "The Little Tadpole Looking for Mom", "The Black Cat Sheriff", "The Calabash Brothers", and many others, which are deeply loved by audiences.
