《Snipe and Mussel》

Director: Hu Jinqing

Introduction of the movie

    Lamau paper-cut animation 'Snipe and Mussel' tells the story of 'Snipe and mussel fight for loach and get hurt by the mussel; Snipe retaliate but get caught by the mussel and hard to break free, finally caught by the fisherman'.

    The film, with its rich ethnic style, delicate and interesting actions, and implicit philosophy, gives the audience a fresh, elegant, meaningful and chic artistic enjoyment.
