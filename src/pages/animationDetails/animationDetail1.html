《The Legend of Sealed Book》

Director: Wang Shuchen / Qian Yunda

Screenplay: Bao Lei / Wang Shuchen

Introduction of the movie

    In the Heavenly Court, there is a precious book called 'The Book of Heaven', which has been locked in a stone niche for 3,000 years. Yuan Gong, the guardian of the Book of Heaven, was curious about the contents of the Book of Heaven, and he took advantage of no one's presence to secretly take it out and read it. It turned out that the Book of Heaven recorded 108 spells, and Yuan Gong secretly descended to the earth and carved the contents of the Book of Heaven on the stone wall. However, Yuan Gong, who intended to benefit mankind, broke the rules of heaven and was punished by the Jade Emperor.

    The elixir in the alchemy furnace was stolen and eaten by three foxes, who turned into spirits and came down to earth to cheat people of their money. They also pretended to be immortal nuns and colluded with the government, oppressing the people and inflicting harm on the earth. So Lord Yuan guides the gifted Egg Sheng, a child who jumps out of an egg, to practise the spells in the Heavenly Book and fight with the foxes to rid the people of evils.

The Jade Rabbit Spirit: A Cunning and Deceptive Villain in "Havoc in Heaven"

The Jade Rabbit Spirit, the primary antagonist in the Chinese animated film "Havoc in Heaven," is a cunning and deceptive villain who embodies greed, ambition, and a thirst for power. Her manipulative nature and relentless pursuit of her goals make her a formidable opponent for the film's protagonist, Egg-Born.

Personality
  • Cunning: The Jade Rabbit Spirit is highly intelligent and resourceful, using her wit and charm to manipulate others and achieve her goals.
  • Deceptive: The Jade Rabbit Spirit is a master of disguise and trickery, often using illusions and false promises to deceive her victims.
  • Ambitious: The Jade Rabbit Spirit is driven by an insatiable desire for power and control, seeking to rule over the heavens and the mortal world.
  • Ruthless: The Jade Rabbit Spirit is willing to do whatever it takes to achieve her goals, showing no mercy or compassion for those who stand in her way.

The Jade Rabbit Spirit steals the Heavenly Book, a powerful tome that holds the key to controlling the heavens and the mortal world. With the book in her possession, she unleashes chaos and destruction, seeking to overthrow the celestial order and establish her own reign.
